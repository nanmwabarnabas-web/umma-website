Constitution of the Unified Merry Minds Association

Amended by: The Executives of the Unified Merry Minds Association on 12th July, 2025.

1. Preamble

We, the members of the Unified Merry Minds Association, having agreed to form ourselves into one body...

2. Citation

This body shall be known as THE UNIFIED MERRY MINDS ASSOCIATION.

3. Aim

The aim of the Association shall be to promote unity, social welfare, and mutual development among members.

4. Motto

The motto of the Association shall be: ‚ÄúGrowth, Welfarism and Socialization.‚Äù

5. Mission & Vision

Mission: To foster cooperation, social integration, and sustainable growth.

Vision: To be a model association of unity and mutual support in Nigeria.

6. Objectives

  • To encourage savings and financial discipline.
  • To support members in times of need.
  • To provide opportunities for skill development and empowerment.
  • To promote harmony and peaceful coexistence.

7. Greeting

The official greeting of the Association shall be determined by the members and used at all gatherings.

8. Supremacy

This Constitution shall be the supreme guiding document of the Association, subject only to amendments duly adopted by members.

9. Membership

Membership shall be open to all individuals who accept the values, rules, and obligations of the Association.

10. Officers

The Association shall have the following officers: Chairman, Secretary, Treasurer, Financial Secretary, and other positions as approved.

11. Vacancy/Removal

Vacancy in any office shall be filled through election. An officer may be removed for misconduct or failure to perform duties.

12. Committees

Standing and ad-hoc committees may be established to oversee specific functions of the Association.

13. Finance

The Association shall be funded by membership dues, levies, donations, and other lawful means.

14. Meetings

Meetings shall be held regularly as determined by the executives. Emergency meetings may be called when necessary.

15. Quorum

A quorum shall consist of not less than one-third of registered members present at a meeting.

16. Absenteeism

Members who are absent without valid reason may be subject to fines or disciplinary measures as agreed by the Association.

17. Standing Order

The Association shall adopt a standing order to regulate proceedings during meetings and official functions.

18. Miscellaneous

All matters not expressly covered by this Constitution shall be referred to the Executives and the general house for decision.

19. Amendment

This Constitution may be amended by a two-thirds majority vote of members present at a duly convened meeting.
