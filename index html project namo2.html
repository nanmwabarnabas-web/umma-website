📜 1. Preamble ▼

We, the members of UMMA, having agreed to form ourselves into one body, establish this constitution...

📝 2. Citation ▼

This body shall be known as THE UNIFIED MERRY MINDS ASSOCIATION (UMMA).

🎯 3. Aim ▼

The aim of the Association is to promote unity, welfare, and mutual development among members.

💡 4. Motto ▼

“Growth, Welfarism and Socialization.”

🚀 5. Mission & Vision ▼

Mission: Foster cooperation, social integration, and sustainable growth.
Vision: To be a model association of unity and mutual support.

🎯 6. Objectives ▼

  • Encourage savings and financial discipline
  • Support members in times of need
  • Provide skill development opportunities
  • Promote harmony and peaceful coexistence
